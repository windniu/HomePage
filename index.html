About

I am now a third-year PhD student at CASIA (Institute of Automation Chinese Academy of Sciences) in Beijing, China. My current research focuses on Processing Natural Language Text and Understanding Physical Signals (e.g., ECG) in the health field. I am passionate about combining deep neural network with human expert knowledge. My PhD advisor is Prof. Wensheng Zhang.
